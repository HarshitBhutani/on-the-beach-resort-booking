BEACHFRONT ACCOMMODATION
Enjoy the breathtaking Noosa beach views directly from your room.

On The Beach Noosa Resort Accommodation offers a great range of fully self-contained apartments and penthouses located in the heart of Hastings St and overlooking Noosa’s Main Beach. Enjoy direct beach access to one of the Sunshine Coast’s best beaches, as well as easy access to cosmopolitan Hastings St with its trendy bars, alfresco cafes, upmarket boutiques and award winning restaurants


. Our larger beachfront and beach view One Bedroom, One Bedroom Penthouse or Two Bedroom Apartments cater to families. These feature separate open-plan living, well-equipped kitchen, quality glassware and crockery, spacious private spa balconies and rooftop oasis, as well as separate, luxe, comfortable bedroom/bathroom areas with varied bedding configurations and options for additional rollaway beds and/or cots


Beachfront Studio or Studio Penthouse Apartments are perfect for singles and couples looking to relax and recharge in Noosa. They offer open plan living including a comfortable lounge area, small well-equipped kitchen and king bed in the combined bedroom/bathroom area. All apartments have flat-screen TV, Foxtel and complimentary WiFi..
