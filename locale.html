LOCALE

A sleek but relaxed modern Italian restaurant Locales layout and design has an elegant feel, combining alfresco eating overlooked by lush Noosa greenery with a sophisticated dark interior featuring a massive timber bar designed to encourage long nights sharing food, wine and conversation. With a focus on quality, handmade dishes, Locale brings a taste of Italy to Hastings Street. The menu has a strong focus on local produce and simple yet stunning flavours, and diners will always experience exceptional service.
