About us

Located on the beach side of Hastings Street, Noosa Heads, On The Beach Noosa lives up
to its name, offering absolute beachfront, luxury apartment accommodation.

A stand-out feature is the private balcony and rooftop spas and dedicated alfresco dining
areas overlooking Noosa’s magnificent Main Beach, North Shore and National Park. It’s a
touch of luxe and a whole load of relaxed.


One of Noosa’s signature beachfront properties – our interior design takes inspiration
from its coastal Noosa surrounds - cabana style retreats, sun lounges, BBQs and spas
invite you to immerse yourself in Noosa’s unique tropical blend of sea, forest and sky.
With direct beach access from the resort, it’s just a few steps to the patrolled main beach
and the sand between your toes.
